This is Faux-llotaxis! This tool uses the concept of Phyllotaxis to generate all sorts of very unique shapes! This tool adds some functions that wouldn't make too much sense in real life, but they certainly look very cool. For example you can change how the points diverge outside of the typical phyllotaxis divergence function, such as changing how the points will move vertically and horizontally as the n, or iteration, value is increased over time.
